# Kiekko: Learn Finnish - Support

Kiekko teaches Finnish through a hockey story with tap-to-translate reading,
audio, and practice games. Core lessons and saved progress work offline; the
free app uses occasional interstitial ads.

## Get help

Questions, bug reports, or feedback: email **caden@cadenwurzbacher.com** and you will get a reply within a few days.

## Privacy

Learning progress stays on your device. Google advertising services may process
limited device and usage data as explained in the full [privacy policy](/kiekko-site/privacy/).
