# Kiekko: Learn Finnish - Support

Kiekko is a fully offline app that teaches Finnish through a 30-chapter hockey story with tap-to-translate reading, audio, and practice games.

## Get help

Questions, bug reports, or feedback: email **cadenwurzbacher@gmail.com** and you will get a reply within a few days.

## Privacy

Kiekko collects no data at all. Read the full [privacy policy](/kiekko-site/privacy/).
